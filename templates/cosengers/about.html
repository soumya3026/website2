Computer Science Engineers

Welcome to COSCENGERS!!!

COSCENGERS is student Association club of Computer Science department wherein the activities are organized by the members of the club who are elected by the students annually. This club is to bring together all students of 2nd ,3rd and final year of CSE.

COSCENGERS means COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERS started in the year 2010 under the leadership of Shakiya Khatun, the president of the first student association.

Every year the students of 2nd,3rd, and 4th year of CSE elect a body with President from final year, Vice president, Secretary, Treasurer from third year. The elected body takes up the charge and organize several events throughout the academic year.

The main motive of the club is to improve student’s communication skills, creativity and teach them how to work effectively with other students. This improves student’s self- esteem and promotes healthy hobbies. Several activities of various categories are conducted in this club which helps students in developing real world skills. And not only it benefits students but also provides a platform to identify and expose student’s interests and talents.
