About Our Club

The Programming Club of GVPCEW is proposed with the overt aim to develop the coding faculties of students by encouraging them to give a stage to implement their ideologies through various activities.
The campaigns of this club are planned to test the independent intellects of the members and the clique as a whole and impart these values to the student community.
The capabilities inherent in the students need to be inhabitantly brought out to give flair to their practical aptitude.


Our Objectives

The objectives of the Programming club include
  1. Regulation of programming practice sessions by final year students to their juniors weekly.
  2. Administration of Python Programming training to other department students by CSE final years.
  3. Inform the community of ongoing global and national coding events such as hackathons, fests and talks.
  4. Organize regular contests to refine potential of the members.
  5. Appraise cognitive capabilities by assigning a problem periodically through Discord.
